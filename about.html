About

Hello there. I'm a Computer Science student at Georgia Tech, with a concentration in the Intelligence and Devices threads (Rogue AIs are more fun when they can control hardware). I'm interested in NLP (especially transformer architectures), reinforcement learning, and robotics.

I am currently working in the NLP and Computational Social Science Lab. In addition, I work in the Automated Algorithm Design Vertically Integrated Project group to optimize algorithms using genetic programming. I'm also a Student Assistant for the Career Services department of the Office of Outreach, Enrollment, and Community in the College of Computing. I have previously worked in the Georgia Tech High-Performace Architecture Lab to adapt conversational and speech-to-text models on IoT devices and robots.

I'm the Executive Officer for The Agency , Georgia Tech's AI club, where I'm working on a project in natural language generation and helping people learn more about the field. Other interests include losing all my money algo-trading, road cycling, and helping find the Ultimate Question of Life, the Universe and Everything .
